Dental Technical Laboratory CMLabs

Laboratory CMLabs specializes only in orthopedic works, it collaborates with private dentists and clinics as well as with other dental technicians.

Extensive experience with modern materials of world producers (BEGO, VITA, GC, Ivoclar, Interdent, Vertex, Yeti ...), our employees regularly undergo training courses (including Hungary, Germany, Slovenia).

We work to be appreciated and guarantee the high quality of our services, all the designs have a 24 calendar months guarantee. We provide an individual approach to each case and the possibility of manufacturing operations in shorter time.

Our values are honesty, quality, trust, responsibility and professionalism. We aim to transform dentistry into the art.

We offer:

-metal and metal-free designs

-removable dental prosthesis

-complex works

-CAD/CAM

-complex laboratory, full cycle of works

-more than15 years of successful work

-all types of orthopedic works

-experienced dental technicians

-high-quality materials and modern equipment

-loyal prices

-individual approach
